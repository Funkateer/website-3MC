About me

'I am a reliable groove supplier'


It all started a night in Tripoli

I was born some years ago on scorching hot day of August in Tripoli (Libya) and almost immediately realized from my hand gestures that I was half Italian, Sicilian to be exact! So I can say I'm the union of the Mediterranean sea and I dig it!

After I finished school in Libya I went off to Sicily because teenagers want to be rebels and rebels weren't very welcome back then!
I first learned some guitar while in Sicily with poor results and unfortunately my musical journey met its end before it even bloomed. I started instead my wanderings which brought me to many places, starting with Canada, where I learned English. I still dream of the British Columbia's mountains!


Bologna

I went back to Italy where I started studying Political Sciences in Bologna, these were intense years to put it mildly crushed between the studies the financial struggle and the uprising war in Libya, everything started to be blurry!
But I guess its true, there is always some light sporadically and that light for me was John W. Coltrane!
A night I entered a Jazz club in Bologna and when I left I was transformed, little I knew about that epiphany! It felt like being on a mission, a mission to turn my life around, a mission that finally finds who the heck I am!



Paris

The rebirth
I know it's the biggest cliche to say but my memories of Paris are all about love 'aaaah Paris l'amour le cliche' Love for the wonderful city, love for the music but must of all the love of my life, my wife!
In Paris I started my real musical journey, it is there where I first started learning music and decided to get serious about it, well I was 29 so I guess it was about the time to get serious, isn't it? yes, but it was also a bit late! So all those insecurities started crippling in! but also the snarky attitude of proving everybody wrong! I decided to debunk the mysticism around music, the 'gotta burn talented or leave' shenanigans! Only hard work setts you apart ... and work hard I started!


Barcelona

All noises at once, like and explosion of sound!
Barcelona was the playground where I did my very first baby steps toward the STAGE, that mythical place, the sweet final lap! I played a lot in that period, I gigged in clubs, I recorded demos and tracks for artists, I started a hip hop duo with a french singer and I had the fist taste of the music production and business



I am currently living in Munich, yes I moved again I told you I am a roamer!
I'm still playing, teaching but most of all learning music everyday all day
