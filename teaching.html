Teaching

I believe that sharing knowledge is the most powerful thing you can do

Life is hard and music should be fun!

Being a self-taught musician means that I had to work harder. Navigating the sheer amount of information, that more often than not were contradictory, or learning a new concept of theory but at the end you don't see how that can be applied musically, because of all that life is harder for us! But wait why should you do the same annoying work if I already did it and know exactly what is worth focusing on and how to study it!
Here I give you couple of options:

Free Lessons

Head to 3MC my YouTube channel and binge watch all the lessons there for you enjoy, along side you'll find pdf and MusicXML files packed with relevant exercises that gets you playing, exercising and eventually getting better not just watching me talking to you (which I appreciate btw)!!

Private Lessons

You think the topics of my YouTube channel are not enough? maybe you want to ask more questions and have an immediate answer? You believe that no matter how much effort I could make in my video lessons nothing will beat the effectiveness of having a person focused on you that mentor and guides you? well that's a no brainer really! I'm convinced of that myself!
I can offer you tailored lessons that focuses on your needs and desires, in remote or in person we can start today just give me a call or send me a message I'll be glad to help

Get in touch

If you have any question or want to collaborate just drop me a line
